The five different dimensions of self-care include emotional, mental and spiritual, physical, social, and practical aspects. Incorporating activities from each dimension into your life means you’ll be nourishing every aspect in your life and ultimately getting better at self-care.

Emotional Self-Care

Emotional self care is a process in which we bring our awareness to our emotions and take the steps necessary in order to honor and care for these emotions. This is essential for our wellbeing.

Physical Self-Care

Physical self-care includes how you’re fueling your body, how much sleep you’re getting, how much physical activity you are doing, and how well you’re caring for your physical needs.

Social Self-Care

Social self-care involves doing things that nurtures and deepens the relationships with people in your life.

Practical Self-Care

Practical self-care includes focusing on tasks that will help prevent future stressful situations.

Mental Self-Care

Mental self-care also involves doing things that help you stay mentally healthy. Practicing self-compassion and acceptance, for example, helps you maintain a healthier inner dialogue.
