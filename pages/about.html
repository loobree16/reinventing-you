About

Mission Statement


Our mission is to provide support and resources that will empower our visitors in the development of trustworthy self-care skills. In order to counteract the myths, rumors and misinformation found online, our team has designed a reliable resource to educate our audience on self-care best practices.


Who We Are


The Reinventing You team is composed of a diverse group of individuals with a wide range of research skills. Each person’s ability to search for, locate, extract, organize, and evaluate information helps the team to create a respected resource and content for self-care.

The five different dimensions of self-care include emotional, mental and spiritual, physical, social, and practical aspects. Incorporating activities from each dimension into your life means you’ll be nourishing every aspect in your life and ultimately getting better at self-care.
